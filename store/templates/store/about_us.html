About Our Team

Welcome to our ecommerce platform, brought to you by a dynamic team of students pursuing advanced studies in "Data Science for Economics and Finance" at the Faculty of Legal, Social, and Economic Sciences in Tangier, Morocco. Nestled at the crossroads of data-driven insights and financial acumen, our project reflects the culmination of our interdisciplinary expertise. As students navigating the realms of law, social sciences, and economics, we've harnessed our collective knowledge to develop a Django-based ecommerce website. From the vibrant city of Tangier, we invite you to explore our platform, where technology, economics, and culture converge to redefine the online shopping experience.
